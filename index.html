A diversity-based education consulting company.

EdEx has substantial experience in providing workshops, presentations, assessments, and other forms of consulting for both professional and parental development in the areas of diversity. We believe that schools communities should celebrate every student for who they are, free from all forms of discrimination. Everyone deserves a place that centers everyone.

What Do We Do?

Framework Building

We work with schools in helping with framing the conversation about diversity, equity, and multicultural education. Through workshops, we introduce different frameworks to approach the conversation. We encourage participation with all stakeholders involved.

Curriclum/Pedagogy

We are typically asked to work with curriculum developers and teachers in the process of developing inclusive instruction and practices. We also find ways to do so even within the context of the standardization of curriculum and high-stakes testing.

Issue-Specific

Are there specific issues that your organizations are facing? We’re able to work with you to think through conducting workshops focused on issues that might be affecting the student experience such as issues of equity, or bias. We aim to broaden our participants understanding and how they might contribute to these systems of inequity.

Professional Development

Where do you go from here? How do you continually help with breaking down the barriers of injustice and systematic racism with your students? We offer trainings on thinking through how you can continually become an advocate for your students and how to invite them in, instead of out.

Diversity Assessment

We work with the school leaders to think through creating a community and diversity assessment and then working with focus groups to help implement plans that will help build up the community or school.
